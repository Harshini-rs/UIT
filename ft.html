Why us?

"The more you save food... The more you can serve others- The more you waste food... The more you make others worry!!"
India has always been a haven of both wealth and starvation. While many are fortunate enough to live well in society, many are ruled against such privilages including food and basic clothing.
The 2017 Global Hunger Index (GHI) Report by IFPRI ranked India 100th out of 118 countries with a serious hunger situation.
The 2019 Global Hunger Index (GHI) report ranked India 102nd out of 117 countries with a serious issue of child wasting. At least one in five children under the age of five years in India are wasted.

So we though of developing a platform where these extremes can meet. We make the food providers meet the needy people and help in making everyone stay happy
